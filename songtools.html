What kind of ads does it create?
Our AI will create various different ad creatives with the assets you upload and use multivariate testing in order to find a winning combination for your SongFly campaign. You can view our creative guidelines webpage to get examples of the ads that will be created.
Does my content get deleted if I cancel my membership?
Your content will be held for 1 year after you cancel your membership, in case you decide to come back. After that point if you do not re-activate your membership, they will be deleted forever.

Our dashboard has a place for your song’s audio files, press photos, promo videos, and keeps them safe, so you can easily access them from anywhere in the future.
